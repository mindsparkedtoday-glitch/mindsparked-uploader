MindSparked Uploader — Privacy Policy

  1. Data Collection: MindSparked Uploader does not collect, store, or sell personal information. All authentication is handled directly through TikTok’s secure OAuth process.
  2. Video Content: Videos are uploaded directly from the user’s device or automation workflow to TikTok via the TikTok API. No video data is stored by MindSparked Uploader.
  3. Third Parties: This app communicates only with TikTok’s official API. No other third-party services are used.
  4. Security: OAuth tokens are stored securely in the user’s own n8n environment. We do not access or control them.
  5. Policy Updates: This Privacy Policy may change as the service evolves. Updates will be reflected in this document.
